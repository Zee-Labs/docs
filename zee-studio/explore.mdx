The **Explore** gallery is a public feed of AI-generated images and videos created by the Postzee community. Discover inspiring content, save your favorites, and use community creations as a starting point for your own.

## How to access

1. Open **Zee Studio** from the sidebar
2. Click the **Explore** tab at the top of the gallery area

## Featured content

At the top of the Explore gallery, a rotating **Featured** carousel highlights curated AI creations. These showcase the best of what's possible with Postzee's AI models:

- Automatically cycles every 5 seconds
- Navigate with arrow buttons or dot indicators
- Click **Use as Base** to start a new generation inspired by a featured item

## Browsing the gallery

The Explore gallery displays community content in an infinite-scroll grid. As you scroll down, more content loads automatically.

### Filtering and sorting

| Filter | Options |
|--------|---------|
| **Type** | All, Images, Videos |
| **Model** | Filter by the AI model used for generation |
| **Sort** | Newest, Popular, Most Viewed |
| **Search** | Full-text search across prompts and descriptions |

## Interacting with content

When you find something interesting in the Explore gallery, you can:

| Action | What it does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Like** | Show appreciation — the like count is visible to everyone |
| **Save to Favorites** | Add to your personal **Favorites** tab for easy access later |
| **Copy to Account** | Clone the media to your **My Creations** library |
| **Use as Base** | Load the creation's prompt and settings into the creator to generate your own variation |

## Favorites tab

Items you save from Explore appear in the **Favorites** tab:

- Access it by clicking the **Favorites** tab at the top of the gallery
- Browse and manage your saved items
- Remove items from favorites at any time

## Publishing your own creations

Share your AI-generated content with the community:

1. Go to **My Creations**
2. Click on an image or video you want to share
3. Click **Publish**
4. Your creation becomes visible in the Explore gallery

You can unpublish at any time to remove it from the public gallery.

<Note>
Only AI-generated content can be published to Explore. Manually uploaded files in My Media are not eligible for the public gallery.
</Note>

## Community guidelines

Content published to the Explore gallery should be:

- **Original** — created using Postzee's AI tools
- **Appropriate** — suitable for a general audience
- **Quality** — represent the best of your AI creations
