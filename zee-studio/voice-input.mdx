Dictate your prompts by voice instead of typing. Tap the microphone icon, speak your idea, and Postzee transcribes it into the prompt field — ready to generate or enhance.

## How to use voice input

1. Open **Zee Studio** and select any creation mode (**AI Image**, **AI Video**, or **Create Post**)
2. Tap the **microphone icon** (🎤) in the creator panel
3. **Allow microphone access** when prompted by your browser (first time only)
4. **Speak your prompt** — a red recording indicator shows you're being recorded
5. When you're done speaking, tap the microphone icon again to stop
6. Wait 1-2 seconds while the audio is transcribed — an amber indicator shows transcription is in progress
7. Your spoken words appear in the prompt field as text

## Recording states

| State | Indicator | What's happening |
|-------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Ready** | Default microphone icon | Tap to start recording |
| **Recording** | Red pulsing indicator | Your voice is being captured |
| **Transcribing** | Amber animated dots | Audio is being converted to text |

## Works everywhere

Voice input works on both desktop and mobile:

- **Desktop** — click the microphone icon in the creator panel
- **Mobile** — tap the microphone icon (same location, optimized for touch)

<Note>
Your browser must support microphone access. Most modern browsers (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge) support this. Make sure you allow microphone permissions when prompted.
</Note>

## Combine with Prompt Optimizer

Voice input pairs perfectly with the Prompt Optimizer for the fastest workflow:

1. **Speak** your idea casually ("a dog running on the beach at sunset")
2. **Enhance** — click the sparkle button (✨) to transform your casual description into a professional prompt
3. **Generate** — click Generate to create your image or video

This lets you go from idea to generation in seconds without typing a single word.

## Tips

- **Speak naturally** — you don't need to use technical photography terms; the Prompt Optimizer handles that
- **Be specific** — "a golden retriever puppy playing on a tropical beach" gives better results than "a dog"
- **One idea at a time** — describe a single scene or concept per recording
- **Quiet environment** — recording in a quiet space improves transcription accuracy
