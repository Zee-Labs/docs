Generate professional AI images directly in Zee Studio. Choose from 16 models optimized for different styles — photorealism, logos, vector art, illustrations, and more.

## How to generate an image

1. Open **Zee Studio** from the top navigation bar (click **Zee Studio**)
2. Select **AI Image** mode in the creator panel
3. Choose your generation mode:
   - **Text to Image** — describe what you want in the prompt field
   - **Image to Image** — attach a reference image and describe the changes you want
4. Type your prompt (e.g., "A professional headshot of a confident woman in a modern office")
5. *(Optional)* Click the **Enhance with AI** button (✨) to optimize your prompt — this dramatically improves results
6. Click the model chip to select an AI model and adjust settings
7. Check the credit cost displayed on the **Generate** button
8. Click **Generate**
9. Wait 10-30 seconds — your image appears in **Creations** when ready

## Model settings

Click the model chip (e.g., **Nano Banana Pro**) to open the settings panel:

- **Model selector** — browse and switch between available models
- **Aspect ratio** — choose between landscape (16:9), portrait (9:16), square (1:1), and other formats
- **Quality/Speed** — some models offer quality tiers (e.g., GPT Image: Low, Medium, High)
- **Style** — some models support style presets (e.g., Ideogram: realistic, design, 3D, anime)
- **Negative prompt** — describe what you don't want in the image (supported by some models)

## Using reference images

In **Image to Image** mode:

1. Click the image icon in the creator panel to attach a reference image
2. The AI model will use your image as a starting point
3. Describe what changes you want in the prompt (e.g., "Transform this into a watercolor painting")

<Note>
Not all models support reference images. Check the model settings panel — models with reference support show an image attachment option.
</Note>

## Generating multiple images at once

Need variations of the same prompt? Generate up to **4 images at once** in a single click.

1. In the creator panel, set the **quantity** to 2, 3, or 4
2. Click **Generate**
3. All images appear in your gallery as a single batch

Each image counts as one generation — your credits are deducted accordingly. Generating 4 images costs 4× the credit cost of one image.

<Note>
Batch generation is great for choosing the best variation. The AI generates each image independently with the same prompt, so you'll get different interpretations of your idea.
</Note>

## Tips for better results

- **Be specific** — "A golden retriever puppy on a beach at sunset" works better than "dog on beach"
- **Use the Prompt Optimizer** — it adds professional photography details (lighting, camera, composition) automatically
- **Mention the style** — "editorial Vogue photography", "cinematic film still", "minimalist flat design"
- **Specify quality** — "ultra-photorealistic 8K", "professional studio lighting"
- **Try different models** — each model has strengths. Nano Banana excels at photos, Recraft at design, Ideogram at text

## After generation

Once your image is ready, click on it to:

- **Download** — save to your device
- **Create Post** — open the post editor with the image attached
- **Recreate** — reuse the same model and settings with a new prompt
- **Use prompt** — copy the prompt to the creator field
- **Animate** — transform the image into a video (Image to Video mode)
- **Publish** — share to the public Explore gallery
