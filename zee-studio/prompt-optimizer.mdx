The **Prompt Optimizer** transforms your simple descriptions into professional-grade prompts that dramatically improve AI generation results. It's free to use — no credit cost.

## What it does

When you click the Enhance button, the Prompt Optimizer analyzes your description and expands it with:

- **Camera specifications** — lens type, focal length, angle, depth of field
- **Lighting design** — natural light direction, studio setups, golden hour, dramatic shadows
- **Composition** — rule of thirds, leading lines, framing, foreground/background balance
- **Color grading** — palette, tone, saturation, film stock emulation
- **Textures and materials** — surface detail, fabric, skin, metal, glass
- **Artistic style** — photography genre, artistic movement, visual reference
- **Motion details** (for videos) — camera movements, scene transitions, motion dynamics

## How to use it

1. Type a basic prompt in the creator field (e.g., "a cat sitting on a windowsill")
2. Click the **Enhance with AI** button (✨) — the purple sparkle icon next to the prompt field
3. Wait 2-3 seconds while the AI optimizes your prompt
4. The enhanced prompt replaces your original text in the field
5. Review the result and click **Generate**

### Example

**Before (your input):**
> A woman walking in the rain

**After (optimized):**
> A young woman in a tailored trench coat walks through a rain-soaked city street at dusk. Shot with a 50mm lens at f/1.8, shallow depth of field. Soft amber streetlights reflect off wet cobblestones. Cinematic color grading with cool blue shadows and warm amber highlights. Rain drops caught mid-fall with subtle motion blur. Editorial fashion photography style, Vogue-inspired composition with leading lines from the street perspective.

## Model-specific optimization

The Prompt Optimizer tailors its output to the selected AI model:

| Model type | Optimization focus |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **Photorealistic models** (Nano Banana, FLUX) | Technical photography terms, camera settings, lighting setups, color science |
| **Design models** (Recraft, Ideogram) | Visual hierarchy, typography placement, brand-safe compositions, vector clarity |
| **Video models** (Sora 2, Veo 3.1) | Camera movements (pan, tilt, zoom, dolly), scene transitions, motion dynamics, temporal progression |
| **Image-to-Video** | Natural animation of existing elements, camera tracking, atmospheric effects, smooth transitions |

## Cost

The Prompt Optimizer is **completely free**. It does not consume any AI credits. Use it as many times as you want.

<Note>
Credits are only charged when you click **Generate** to create the actual image or video. The prompt enhancement step is always free.
</Note>

## Tips

- **Start simple** — the optimizer works best when you give it a clear, concise idea to expand
- **Don't over-specify** — if you write a long, detailed prompt, the optimizer has less room to add value
- **Review the output** — you can edit the enhanced prompt before generating to fine-tune the result
- **Use it for videos too** — the optimizer adds camera movements and motion descriptions that significantly improve video quality
- **Combine with voice input** — speak your idea using the microphone, then enhance it with the optimizer
