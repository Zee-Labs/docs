Create professional talking-head videos using AI avatars powered by **HeyGen**. Choose from a library of realistic avatars, select a voice, write your script, and generate a video — no camera or microphone needed.

## What are HeyGen Avatars?

HeyGen Avatars are AI-generated video presenters that lip-sync to your script. They look and move like real people, making them ideal for:

- Product demos and explainer videos
- Social media content with a personal touch
- Training and educational videos
- Marketing announcements
- Multilingual content (same avatar, different languages)

## Getting started

### Connect your HeyGen account

Before using avatars, you need to connect your HeyGen account:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations**
2. Find **HeyGen** in the integrations list
3. Click **Connect** and follow the authorization flow
4. Once connected, avatars become available in Zee Studio

<Note>
You need a HeyGen account to use this feature. If you don't have one, you can [sign up at HeyGen](https://www.heygen.com/signup) and then connect it to Postzee.
</Note>

## How to create an avatar video

1. Open **Zee Studio** from the sidebar
2. Select **AI Video** mode in the creator panel
3. Choose **HeyGen Avatar** as the generation type
4. **Select an avatar**:
   - Browse the avatar gallery
   - Search by name to find specific avatars
   - Hover over an avatar to preview their video
   - Click to select
5. **Select a voice**:
   - Browse available voices
   - Filter by language using the dropdown
   - Click the play button to preview a voice
   - Click to select
6. **Write your script** — type or paste the text your avatar will speak
7. Check the credit cost and click **Generate**
8. Wait for processing — avatar videos typically take 2-5 minutes
9. Your video appears in **My Creations** when ready

## Choosing an avatar

The avatar gallery shows all available HeyGen avatars:

- **Search** — find avatars by name
- **Preview** — hover over an avatar card to see a short video preview
- **Types** — regular avatars and talking photos are available
- **Gender** — browse male and female presenters

Click an avatar to select it. A purple border indicates your selection.

## Choosing a voice

The voice selector lets you find the perfect voice for your content:

- **Language filter** — select a language to show only voices in that language
- **Gender indicators** — each voice shows whether it's female or male
- **Audio preview** — click the play button to hear a sample before selecting
- **Search** — find voices by name

<Note>
For multilingual content, you can use the same avatar with different language voices. The avatar will lip-sync naturally to any supported language.
</Note>

## Tips for better avatar videos

- **Write naturally** — write your script as you would speak it, with pauses and natural phrasing
- **Keep scripts focused** — shorter, focused scripts produce better results than long monologues
- **Match voice to content** — choose a voice that fits your brand tone (professional, friendly, energetic)
- **Test with previews** — always preview both the avatar and voice before generating

## After generation

Once your avatar video is ready, you can:

- **Download** — save the video to your device
- **Create Post** — attach the video to a social media post
- **Publish** — share to the Explore gallery
