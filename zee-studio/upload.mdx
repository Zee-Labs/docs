Upload your own images and videos to Zee Studio. Use them in social media posts, as reference images for AI generation, or simply to keep your media organized in one place.

## How to upload

There are three ways to upload media to Zee Studio:

### Drag and drop

1. Open **Zee Studio** from the top navigation bar (click **Zee Studio**)
2. Switch to the **Files** tab
3. Drag files from your computer directly onto the gallery area
4. A drop zone overlay appears — release to upload
5. Files begin uploading immediately with a progress bar

### Click to browse

1. Click the **upload zone** in the Files tab
2. Your file picker opens — select one or more files
3. Click **Open** to start the upload

### Upload button

1. Click the **upload button** in the gallery toolbar
2. Select files from your device
3. Upload starts automatically

## Supported formats

| Type | Formats | Max size |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Images** | PNG, JPG, JPEG, WEBP | 30 MB per file |
| **Videos** | MP4, MOV | 400 MB per file |

You can upload up to **5 files** at a time.

## Upload progress

During upload, you'll see:

- An animated upload icon
- A progress bar showing upload completion
- "Uploading..." status text

When the upload completes, your files appear immediately in the **Files** tab.

## Error handling

If an upload fails, you'll see a toast notification explaining the issue:

| Error | Cause | Solution |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **Unsupported format** | File type not in the supported list | Convert to PNG, JPG, WEBP, MP4, or MOV |
| **Image too large** | Image exceeds 30 MB | Compress or resize the image |
| **Video too large** | Video exceeds 400 MB | Compress the video or reduce resolution |
| **Too many files** | More than 5 files selected | Upload in batches of 5 or fewer |

## Using uploaded media

Once your files are in **Files**, you can:

- **Create Post** — attach the media to a social media post
- **Image to Image** — use an uploaded image as a reference for AI generation
- **Image to Video** — animate a still image using AI video models
- **Download** — re-download the file to your device
- **Delete** — remove the file from your library

<Note>
Uploaded media is stored securely in the cloud and available from any device where you're logged into Postzee.
</Note>
