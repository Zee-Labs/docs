**Zee Studio** is Postzee's all-in-one content creation hub. Generate AI images, AI videos, create social media posts, manage your media library, and discover community content — all from a single interface.

## How to access

Click **Studio** in the top navigation bar and select **Zee Studio**. You'll see the creator panel at the bottom of the screen with your media gallery above it.

## Creation modes

The creator panel supports three modes, selectable from the top row:

| Mode | What it does |
|------|-------------|
| **AI Image** | Generate images using 16 AI models |
| **AI Video** | Generate videos using models like Sora 2, Veo 3.1, Kling |
| **Create Post** | Write and publish social media posts with text, images, and videos |

Each mode has its own generation options:

- **Text to Image** / **Image to Image** — generate from a text prompt or transform an existing image
- **Text to Video** / **Image to Video** — create a video from scratch or animate a still image

## Gallery tabs

Above the creator panel, your content is organized into tabs:

| Tab | Content |
|-----|---------|
| **Explore** | Public gallery of community-created AI media. Save items to your own library. |
| **Favorites** | Media you've liked or favorited from the Explore gallery. |
| **My Creations** | Images and videos you've generated with AI. |
| **My Media** | Files you've uploaded manually (drag-and-drop or upload button). |

## Key features

- **Prompt Optimizer** — Click the sparkle icon (✨) to automatically enhance your prompt for dramatically better results. Free, no credit cost. [Learn more →](/zee-studio/prompt-optimizer)
- **Voice Input** — Tap the microphone icon to dictate your prompt by voice. Works on mobile and desktop. [Learn more →](/zee-studio/voice-input)
- **Model Settings** — Click the model chip (e.g., "Nano Banana Pro") to open the settings panel where you can switch models, adjust parameters, and see credit costs.
- **Source Images** — In **Image to Image** or **Image to Video** mode, attach a reference image that the AI model will use as a starting point.
- **Credit Cost Preview** — The **Generate** button shows the estimated credit cost before you confirm (e.g., "Generate · 80").

## Workflow: Generate and Post

The typical flow in Zee Studio:

1. Select a mode (**AI Image**, **AI Video**, or **Create Post**)
2. Write your prompt or post content
3. Optionally enhance with the Prompt Optimizer
4. Choose a model and adjust settings
5. Click **Generate** (for AI content) or select channels and click **Schedule** / **Publish now** (for posts)
6. After generation completes, click on the media to view, download, or create a post from it
