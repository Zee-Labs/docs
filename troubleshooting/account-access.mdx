## I forgot my password

1. Go to the login page
2. Click **Forgot your password?**
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your inbox for a password reset link (check spam/junk folder too)
5. Click the link and set a new password

<Note>
The reset link expires in **20 minutes**. If it expired, request a new one.
</Note>

<Warning>
Password reset only works for accounts created with email/password. If you signed up with **Google**, use the "Sign in with Google" button instead — there's no password to reset.
</Warning>

## I can't log in

**Check these common issues:**

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Wrong password | Use the "Forgot your password?" link to reset |
| Wrong email | Make sure you're using the same email you registered with |
| Signed up with Google | Click "Sign in with Google" instead of entering a password |
| Account not activated | Check your email for the activation link (sent during registration) |
| Account blocked | Your payment may have failed — check your email for payment notifications |

## I signed up with Google but want to set a password

If you registered with Google and want to also have a password:

1. Log in with Google
2. Go to **Settings** → **Account**
3. In the password section, set a new password

After this, you can log in with either Google or your email/password.

## I want to change my email

Currently, your email address cannot be changed after account creation. If you need to use a different email:

1. Create a new account with the desired email
2. Contact support to transfer your subscription and data

## I want to delete my account

To delete your account and all associated data, contact support through the chat widget. Include:
- Your account email
- Confirmation that you want all data deleted

We will process your request in accordance with LGPD/GDPR requirements.

## I can't access my team

**If you were invited to a team:**
1. Check your email for the invitation link
2. Click the link to accept the invitation
3. If the link expired, ask the team admin to resend it

**If you're the admin:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Team**
2. View your team members
3. Send new invitations if needed

## My account shows "Activation required"

After registering, you need to activate your account:

1. Check your email inbox (and spam folder) for the activation email
2. Click the activation link
3. If the email didn't arrive, go to the login page and request a new activation email

<Note>
You can request a new activation email up to 3 times. Wait at least 60 seconds between requests.
</Note>

## Still having issues?

Contact support through the chat widget with:
- Your account email
- A description of the issue
- Any error messages you see on the login page
