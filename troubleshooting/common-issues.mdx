## File upload fails

**Check these requirements:**

| File type | Max size | Supported formats |
|-----------|---------|-------------------|
| Images | 10 MB | JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF |
| Videos | 1 GB | MP4 only |

**Common fixes:**
- **Wrong format:** Convert your video to MP4 before uploading. MOV, AVI, and MKV are not supported.
- **File too large:** Compress your image or video before uploading.
- **Storage full:** Check your storage usage in the account menu. Delete old media or upgrade your plan for more space.

## Storage is full

When your storage reaches the limit, you cannot upload new files or generate AI content.

**How to free space:**
1. Go to **Zee Studio** → **Files** tab
2. Delete media you no longer need
3. Storage is freed immediately after deletion

**Storage limits by plan:**

| Plan | Storage |
|------|---------|
| Free | 10 GB |
| Standard | 15 GB |
| Team | 30 GB |
| Pro | 50 GB |
| Ultimate | 100 GB |

To get more storage, upgrade your plan in **Settings** → **Billing**.

## The page is loading forever

**Try these steps in order:**

1. **Hard refresh:** Press `Ctrl+Shift+R` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+R` (Mac)
2. **Clear browser cache:** Go to browser settings → Clear browsing data → Cached images and files
3. **Try a different browser:** If the issue persists, try Chrome, Firefox, or Edge
4. **Check your internet:** Make sure your connection is stable
5. **Disable browser extensions:** Ad blockers or privacy extensions can sometimes interfere

If the problem persists after all these steps, it may be a temporary server issue. Wait a few minutes and try again.

## The calendar doesn't show my posts

**Common causes:**

- **Wrong month/week:** Navigate to the correct date using the calendar controls
- **Filters active:** Check if any filters are applied (platform, status) and clear them
- **Channel not selected:** Make sure the relevant channel is selected in the sidebar filter

## Notifications are not appearing

- **Check the bell icon** in the top navigation bar — the badge shows unread count
- **Open the notification drawer** by clicking the bell
- If the drawer shows empty, close and reopen it — notifications refresh when the drawer opens
- Make sure notification emails are enabled in **Settings** → **Account** → **Email preferences**

## Analytics show zero data

**Common causes:**

- **New channel:** Analytics data takes 24-48 hours to appear after connecting a channel
- **No published posts:** Analytics only show data for published posts
- **Platform delay:** Some platforms (especially TikTok and LinkedIn) take longer to provide analytics data

## I can't see a feature mentioned in the docs

Some features are available only on specific plans:

| Feature | Required plan |
|---------|--------------|
| Social media suite (channels, calendar, publishing) | Standard+ |
| AI image/video generation | All plans (Free requires purchasing credits) |
| Team members | Team+ |
| API access | Standard+ |
| Auto-recharge | Standard+ |

If you're on the Free plan, you can access AI features by purchasing credit packages, but you cannot connect channels or schedule posts.

## The dark/light theme doesn't look right

1. Click your **avatar** in the top right
2. Click the **appearance settings** icon (paint palette)
3. Choose between **Light**, **Dark**, or **System** mode

If the theme doesn't apply immediately, try refreshing the page.

## I have an issue not listed here

Contact support through the chat widget (available on desktop). When contacting support, include:
- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead
- Any error messages you see
- Your browser and device (e.g., Chrome on Mac)

Our support team typically responds within a few hours during business hours.
