All Postzee payments are processed securely through **Stripe**. Prices are in **USD** regardless of your location.

## My credit purchase didn't appear

**Cause:** The payment may still be processing, or the checkout session didn't complete.

**Fix:**
1. Check your email for a payment confirmation from Stripe
2. Click your **profile picture** (top right) → **Credits** — your balance should update within a few seconds
3. If no confirmation email arrived, the payment likely didn't go through — try again
4. If you received a confirmation but credits don't appear, wait a few minutes and refresh the page

<Note>
Credits appear instantly after successful payment. If they don't show after 5 minutes and you have a payment confirmation, contact support with your email so we can verify.
</Note>

## My card was declined

**Cause:** Your bank or card issuer rejected the transaction. Common reasons:
- Insufficient funds
- International transaction blocked (Postzee charges in USD)
- Card expired
- Daily spending limit reached

**Fix:**
1. Contact your bank to authorize international USD transactions
2. Try a different card
3. Ensure your card is enabled for online/international purchases

## I was charged but my subscription didn't activate

**Cause:** There may have been a delay between payment and account activation.

**Fix:**
1. Log out and log back in — your plan may need a session refresh
2. Check **Billing** in settings to verify your subscription status
3. If still showing Free after 10 minutes, contact support with your email

## How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Click your **profile picture** (top right) to open the account menu
2. Click **Billing**
3. Go to the **Overview** tab
4. Click **Cancel Subscription**
5. Follow the prompts to confirm

**What happens after cancellation:**
- You keep access until the end of the current billing period
- **Monthly AI credits** are removed at the end of the period
- **Purchased credits** (bought separately) are **never removed** — they stay in your account forever
- You can still use purchased credits on the Free plan

## How do I change my payment card?

1. Click your **profile picture** (top right) to open the account menu
2. Click **Billing**
3. Go to the **Overview** tab
4. Click **Update Payment**
5. You'll be redirected to the Stripe portal to update your card details

If your subscription payment failed:
1. Update your card using the steps above
2. Stripe will automatically retry the payment with your new card
3. Once the payment succeeds, your account is unblocked automatically

## Why was my account blocked?

Your account may be blocked if:
- **Payment failed** — your subscription payment didn't go through. Update your card and the account is unblocked automatically when payment succeeds.
- **Chargeback dispute** — if you disputed a charge with your bank, the account is blocked until the dispute is resolved.

## I want a refund

- **Subscription refunds:** Contact support with your account email. Refund eligibility depends on your usage and billing cycle.
- **Credit purchases:** Credits are non-refundable once used. Unused credit balances may be eligible for refund — contact support.
- **Failed AI generations:** Credits are **automatically refunded** when an AI generation fails. You don't need to request this — it happens instantly.

## Why am I charged in USD?

Postzee processes all payments in **US Dollars (USD)**. Your bank may convert the amount to your local currency and apply a conversion fee. This is standard for international SaaS platforms and ensures consistent pricing worldwide.

## My trial ended but I don't want to pay

During the **7-day free trial**, you have full access to the plan features except AI credits (credits are only granted after first payment). When the trial ends:
- If you don't provide payment → subscription is cancelled, you return to the Free plan
- If you provide payment → first charge occurs and you receive your monthly AI credits

You can cancel before the trial ends to avoid being charged.

## Still having issues?

Contact support through the chat widget with:
- Your account email
- The type of issue (payment, subscription, credits)
- Any error messages you see
