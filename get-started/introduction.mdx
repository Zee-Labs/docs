Whether you're a content creator, marketing team, or agency managing multiple accounts, Postzee gives you the tools to create, schedule, and analyze your social media content effortlessly.

## Supported platforms

Postzee supports **30+ social networks** including Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, X (Twitter), LinkedIn, YouTube, Pinterest, Threads, Bluesky, Mastodon, Discord, Slack, Telegram, Reddit, and more.

## Key features

- **AI-Powered Content** — Generate images and videos with 20+ AI models, including Nano Banana, FLUX, Recraft, GPT Image, Sora 2, Veo 3.1, and Kling
- **Prompt Optimizer** — Automatically enhance your prompts for dramatically better AI results (powered by Claude AI)
- **Visual Calendar** — Drag-and-drop scheduling with week and month views
- **Multi-Channel Publishing** — Post to multiple social networks simultaneously with platform-specific settings
- **Team Collaboration** — Invite team members, manage roles, and collaborate on content
- **AI Credits System** — Pay-as-you-go credits for AI generation, with monthly plan allocations
- **Analytics Dashboard** — Track engagement, followers, and content performance
- **Public API & Integrations** — REST API, MCP server for AI agents, and n8n workflow automation
- **4 Languages** — Full interface available in English, Portuguese, Spanish, and French
