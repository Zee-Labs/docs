Get up and running with Postzee in 5 minutes. This guide walks you through creating your account, connecting a social channel, generating AI content, and publishing your first post.

## 1. Create your account

1. Go to [dashboard.postzee.app](https://dashboard.postzee.app)
2. Sign up with your email or social login (Google, GitHub)
3. Choose your plan — you can start with **Free** to explore AI features, or select a paid plan (**Standard**, **Team**, **Pro**, or **Ultimate**) to unlock the full social media suite

<Note>
New paid plans include a **7-day free trial**. No charge during the trial period — cancel anytime.
</Note>

## 2. Connect your first channel

1. Click **Channels** in the top navigation bar
2. Click **Add Channel**
3. Select your social platform (e.g., Instagram, LinkedIn, X)
4. Complete the OAuth authorization to grant Postzee access
5. Your channel appears in the **Active Channels** tab

<Note>
You need at least one connected channel to publish posts. You can connect multiple accounts across different platforms.
</Note>

## 3. Generate an AI image

1. Click **Studio** in the top navigation bar and select **Zee Studio**
2. You'll see the creator panel at the bottom — select **AI Image** mode
3. Type a prompt describing what you want (e.g., "A golden retriever wearing sunglasses at the beach")
4. Click the **Enhance with AI** button (sparkle icon) to automatically optimize your prompt for better results
5. Choose an AI model (e.g., **Nano Banana Pro** for photorealism or **Recraft V4** for design)
6. Click **Generate**
7. Wait 10-30 seconds — your image appears in the gallery when ready

<Note>
Each generation costs AI credits. Check your balance in the account drawer (click your avatar in the top-right). Free plan users can purchase credits to use AI features.
</Note>

## 4. Create and publish a post

1. Once your image is generated, click on it in the gallery
2. Click **Create Post** from the media actions
3. Write your post caption in the text editor — or click the sparkle icon to **generate a caption with AI**
4. Select which channels to publish to
5. Choose when to publish:
   - **Publish now** — posts immediately
   - **Schedule** — pick a date and time
   - **Draft** — save for later
6. Click **Schedule** or **Publish now**

Your post is created! Check the **Calendar** page to see it on your schedule.

## 5. Check your analytics

1. Click **Metrics** in the top navigation bar
2. View engagement rates, follower counts, and post performance across all your connected channels
3. Filter by time period and platform
