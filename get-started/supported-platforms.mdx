## Platform features

All platforms support:
- **Text content** with platform-specific formatting
- **Image attachments** (single or multiple where supported)
- **Scheduling** — publish now or schedule for later
- **Draft** — save content without publishing

Some platforms also support:
- **Video uploads** (Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube, etc.)
- **Carousel posts** (Instagram, LinkedIn)
- **Stories** (Instagram, Facebook)
- **Platform-specific settings** (TikTok privacy, YouTube visibility, etc.)

<Note>
Each platform requires a separate OAuth connection. Go to **Channels** → **Add Channel** to connect your accounts. Some platforms (like Instagram and Facebook) use the same Facebook Business authorization.
</Note>
