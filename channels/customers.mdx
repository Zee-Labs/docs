If you manage social media for multiple clients (agency mode), Postzee lets you organize your channels into **customers**. Each customer is a group of channels belonging to a single client, making it easy to filter, schedule, and report per client.

## What is a customer?

A customer is a logical grouping of channels. For example, if you manage:

- 3 Instagram accounts for **Coffee Brand X**
- 2 LinkedIn pages for **Tech Startup Y**
- 1 TikTok and 1 YouTube for **Restaurant Z**

You can create three customers (one per client) and assign their respective channels to each.

## Creating a customer

1. Go to **Channels** in the top navigation bar
2. Click **Add Customer**
3. Enter the customer name (e.g., "Coffee Brand X")
4. Click **Save**

The customer appears in your customer list, ready to receive channels.

## Assigning channels to a customer

When connecting a new channel:

1. Click **Add Channel**
2. After authorization, select the customer this channel belongs to
3. The channel is now grouped under that customer

For existing channels:

1. Open the channel's settings
2. Click **Customer**
3. Select the customer from the dropdown (or create a new one)
4. Save

## Filtering by customer

Once your channels are organized:

- **Calendar** — filter posts by customer to see only one client's schedule
- **Channels** — filter the channels list by customer
- **Analytics** — view metrics per customer
- **API** — pass `customer` as a query parameter when listing posts

This makes agency-style operations much easier than scrolling through mixed content from all clients.

## Renaming a customer

1. Open the customer in the **Channels** page
2. Click **Edit** next to the customer name
3. Update the name and save

The new name applies everywhere immediately.

## Deleting a customer

1. **Reassign or delete all channels** under the customer first (a customer cannot be deleted with active channels)
2. Open the customer
3. Click **Delete**
4. Confirm

Posts published in the past remain on their respective platforms — only the customer grouping is removed in Postzee.

## Plan availability

Customers (agency mode) is available on **Team**, **Pro**, and **Ultimate** plans.

<Note>
The number of channels per customer is unlimited within your plan's total channel limit. For example, on the Pro plan (30 channels total), you could have 5 customers with 6 channels each, or 10 customers with 3 channels each.
</Note>
