Complete credit cost reference for all AI models in Postzee. All prices are shown in **credits** (1,000 credits = $1 USD).

<Note>
The exact credit cost for each generation is always shown on the **Generate** button before you confirm. Costs shown here are base values — actual costs may vary slightly by aspect ratio and quality settings.
</Note>

## How pricing works

- **$1 USD = 1,000 credits**
- Credits are deducted when you click **Generate**
- The **Prompt Optimizer** is always free (0 credits)
- Costs vary by model, resolution, quality, and duration (for videos)

## Video model pricing

### Sora 2 — flat per-video pricing

| Model | 10 seconds | 15 seconds |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| **Sora 2** | ~300 credits | ~350 credits |
| **Sora 2 Pro (720p)** | ~1,500 credits | ~2,700 credits |
| **Sora 2 Pro (1080p)** | ~3,300 credits | ~6,300 credits |

Sora 2 Image-to-Video uses the same pricing as Text-to-Video.

**Additional features:**
| Feature | Credit cost |
|---------|-------------|
| Watermark removal | ~100 credits |
| Storyboard (720p, 10s) | ~1,500 credits |
| Storyboard (720p, 15-25s) | ~2,700 credits |

### Veo 3.1 — per-second pricing

#### Veo 3.1 Fast (credits per second)

| Resolution | Without audio | With audio |

### Other video models

Kling, Luma Ray, Seedance, Wan, PixVerse, Vidu, MiniMax, and Hunyuan models use dynamic pricing based on duration and settings. The exact cost is always displayed on the **Generate** button before you confirm.
