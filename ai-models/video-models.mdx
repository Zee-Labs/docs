Postzee offers **30+ AI video models** across multiple families. Generate videos from text prompts or animate still images with models ranging from fast and affordable to cinematic and professional-grade.

## Sora 2 family

Fast, cost-effective video generation with flat per-video pricing.

| Model | Modes | Duration | Resolution | Credit cost |
|-------|-------|----------|------------|-------------|
| **Sora 2** | Text-to-Video, Image-to-Video | 10s, 15s | Standard | ~300-350 credits |
| **Sora 2 Pro (720p)** | Text-to-Video, Image-to-Video | 10s, 15s | 720p HD | ~1,500-2,700 credits |
| **Sora 2 Pro (1080p)** | Text-to-Video, Image-to-Video | 10s, 15s | 1080p Full HD | ~3,300-6,300 credits |

**Best for:** Fast iterations, social media content, cost-effective video production. Pro tier for professional-grade cinematic quality.

### Additional Sora 2 features

- **Storyboard mode** — compose multi-shot videos with scene descriptions and per-shot durations (10s, 15s, 25s total)
- **Watermark removal** — remove watermarks from Sora-generated videos (~100 credits)

## Veo 3.1 family

Google's video generation model. Uses **per-second pricing** that varies by resolution and audio generation.

| Model | Modes | Duration | Best for |
|-------|-------|----------|----------|
| **Veo 3.1** | Text-to-Video, Image-to-Video | 4s, 6s, 8s | Highest quality, smooth and stable visuals |
| **Veo 3.1 Fast** | Text-to-Video, Image-to-Video | 4s, 6s, 8s | Quick iterations, optional synced audio |

### Veo 3.1 per-second pricing

#### Veo 3.1 (Standard)

| Resolution | Without audio | With audio |
|------------|--------------|------------|
| 720p / 1080p | 400 credits/s | 800 credits/s |
| 4K | 800 credits/s | 1,200 credits/s |

#### Veo 3.1 Fast

| Resolution | Without audio | With audio |
|------------|--------------|------------|
| 720p / 1080p | 200 credits/s | 300 credits/s |
| 4K | 600 credits/s | 700 credits/s |

### Example costs (Veo 3.1 Fast, 720p)

| Duration | Without audio | With audio |
|----------|--------------|------------|
| 4 seconds | ~800 credits | ~1,200 credits |
| 6 seconds | ~1,200 credits | ~1,800 credits |
| 8 seconds | ~1,600 credits | ~2,400 credits |

<Note>
Veo 3.1 supports **audio generation** — the AI creates a synchronized soundtrack for your video. This feature is available on both Standard and Fast tiers and increases the per-second cost.
</Note>

## Generation modes

### Text to Video

All video models support **Text to Video** — describe the scene and motion you want, and the AI generates a video from scratch.

### Image to Video

Several models support **Image to Video** — provide a still image and describe the motion you want to add:

| Model | Image-to-Video support |
|-------|:---:|

| Sora 2 Pro | Yes |
