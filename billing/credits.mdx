AI credits are the currency used to generate images and videos in Postzee. Every AI generation costs a specific number of credits depending on the model, resolution, and settings.

## How credits work

**1 dollar = 1,000 credits**

When you generate an image or video, the credit cost is deducted from your balance. The exact cost is always shown on the **Generate** button before you confirm.

## Types of credits

There are two types of credits in Postzee — they behave differently:

| Type | Source | Expiration | On cancellation |
|------|--------|------------|-----------------|
| **Plan credits** | Included monthly with paid plans | Reset every billing cycle | Removed when you cancel |
| **Purchased credits** | Bought separately as credit packages | **Never expire** | **Kept forever** |

### Plan credits

Paid plans include a monthly credit allocation:

| Plan | Monthly Credits |
|------|----------------|
| Standard | 2,000 |
| Team | 3,000 |
| Pro | 5,000 |
| Ultimate | 10,000 |

Plan credits reset at the start of each billing cycle. Unused plan credits do not roll over.

### Purchased credits

Credits bought through credit packages are permanent:

- **Never expire** — use them whenever you want
- **Survive cancellation** — if you cancel your plan, purchased credits remain in your account
- **Available on all plans** — including the Free plan

## Checking your balance

Your current credit balance is visible in the **account drawer** (click your avatar in the top-right corner). It shows both your total available credits and a breakdown by type.

You can also check your detailed balance by clicking the credit display in the account drawer, which takes you to the **Credits** page.

## Credit usage

Each AI generation shows its credit cost before you confirm:

- The **Generate** button displays the estimated cost (e.g., "Generate · 160")
- Credit costs vary by model — some models are more expensive than others
- Higher resolutions, longer durations, and quality settings increase the cost

### Example costs

| Generation | Approximate cost |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Image with Nano Banana (original) | ~78 credits |
| Image with Nano Banana 2 | ~160 credits |
| Image with Nano Banana Pro | ~300 credits |
| Image with GPT Image Mini | ~22 credits |
| Image with Recraft V4 (raster) | ~80 credits |
| Image with FLUX 1.1 Ultra | ~120 credits |
| Image with GPT Image 2 (High) | ~440 credits |
| Video with Sora 2 (10s) | ~300 credits |
| Video with Veo 3.1 Fast (8s, no audio) | ~800 credits |
| Video with Veo 3.1 (8s, no audio) | ~1,600 credits |
| Prompt Optimizer | **Free** (0 credits) |

<Note>
Exact costs depend on the specific model, resolution, quality settings, and duration. Check the Generate button for the precise cost before confirming.
</Note>

See [AI Models Pricing](/ai-models/pricing) for the complete cost reference.

## What doesn't cost credits

These features are always free:

- **Prompt Optimizer** — enhance your prompts at no cost
- **Voice input** — dictate prompts by voice
- **Browsing Explore** — view community gallery
- **Uploading media** — upload your own files to Files
- **Scheduling and publishing posts** — the social media suite uses your plan, not credits
