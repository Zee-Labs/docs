Need more AI credits? Purchase additional credit packages anytime — they never expire and work on every plan, including Free.

## Available credit packages

| Package | Credits | Price |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **Starter** | 2,000 | $2 |
| **Basic** | 10,000 | $10 |
| **Standard** | 25,000 | $25 |
| **Pro** | 50,000 | $50 |
| **Enterprise** | 100,000 | $100 |

All packages use the same rate: **$1 = 1,000 credits**. No minimums, no markups.

## How to purchase

1. Open the account drawer (click your avatar in the top-right) and click the **credit balance**, or go to the **Credits** page directly
2. Browse the available credit packages
3. Click **Buy** on the package you want
4. Complete payment through the secure Stripe checkout
5. Credits are added to your account instantly

## Payment methods

Postzee uses **Stripe** for all payments. Accepted methods include:

- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Other methods supported by Stripe in your region

## Key rules

- **Purchased credits never expire** — use them today, next month, or next year
- **Credits survive plan changes** — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan and your purchased credits remain
- **Available on all plans** — Free plan users can buy credits and use all AI features
- **No refunds on credits** — credit purchases are final

<Note>
Purchased credits are completely separate from your plan's monthly credit allocation. Plan credits reset each billing cycle, but purchased credits are permanent.
</Note>

## When to buy credits

Consider purchasing additional credits when:

- Your monthly plan credits are running low before the billing cycle resets
- You're on the Free plan and want to use AI generation
- You have a large batch of content to generate (campaign, launch)
- You want a permanent credit reserve that won't be affected by plan changes
