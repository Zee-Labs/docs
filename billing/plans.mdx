## Plan details

### Free — $0/month

The Free plan gives you access to Postzee's AI features without any commitment:

- **Generate AI images and videos** by purchasing credit packages
- **Use Zee Studio** — full access to all AI models, Prompt Optimizer, voice input
- **No social media suite** — you cannot connect channels, schedule, or publish posts

The Free plan is ideal for exploring AI generation before committing to a paid plan.

### Standard — $27/month

Everything you need to start managing your social presence:

- **5 channels** — connect up to 5 social media accounts
- **400 posts/month** — schedule and publish up to 400 posts
- **2,000 AI credits/month** — included for image and video generation
- **15 GB storage** — for uploaded media and generated content
- **Public API access** — automate with the REST API
- **Image editor** — built-in Polotno editor for image modifications

### Team — $37/month

For teams that collaborate on content:

- **10 channels** — connect up to 10 accounts
- **Unlimited posts** — no monthly post limit
- **Unlimited team members** — invite your entire team
- **3,000 AI credits/month** — more credits for team generation
- **30 GB storage**

### Pro — $47/month

For professionals managing multiple brands:

- **30 channels** — manage multiple brands and accounts
- **Unlimited posts and team members**
- **5,000 AI credits/month**
- **50 GB storage**

### Ultimate — $97/month

For agencies and power users:

- **100 channels** — manage large portfolios of accounts
- **Unlimited everything** — posts, team members, features
- **10,000 AI credits/month**
- **100 GB storage**

## Free plan and AI features

Users on the **Free plan can purchase AI credits** and use all AI features:

- Image generation with 16 models and 30+ video models
- Video generation with all video models
- Prompt Optimizer (always free, no credits needed)
- Voice input
- Zee Studio (full access)

The only restriction is no access to the social media suite (channels, scheduling, publishing, calendar, analytics).

<Note>
Free users can see all premium features in the interface with contextual upgrade prompts. Nothing is hidden — you can explore the full product before upgrading.
</Note>

## Changing plans

You can upgrade, downgrade, or switch billing periods at any time from the **Billing** page (open the account drawer → **Subscription**):

- **Upgrade** — takes effect immediately, prorated for the remaining billing period
- **Downgrade** — takes effect at the end of your current billing period
- **Monthly ↔ Yearly** — prorated automatically

See [Managing Your Subscription](/billing/managing-subscription) for details.
