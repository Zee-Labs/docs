Invite team members to collaborate on your social media content. Assign roles to control what each person can access and manage.

## How to access

Go to **Settings** → **Team** in the sidebar.

## Plans with team support

| Plan | Team members |
|------|-------------|
| **Free** | — |
| **Standard** | — |
| **Team** | Unlimited |
| **Pro** | Unlimited |
| **Ultimate** | Unlimited |

<Note>
Team members are available on **Team**, **Pro**, and **Ultimate** plans. If you're on Standard or Free, upgrade to Team or higher to invite collaborators.
</Note>

## Inviting team members

1. Go to **Settings** → **Team**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter the person's **email address**
4. Select a **role** (User or Admin)
5. Choose whether to **send an email invitation**
6. Click **Invite**

The invited person receives an email with a link to join your team. Alternatively, you can copy the invite link and share it directly.

## Roles

Each team member has a role that controls their permissions:

| Role | Permissions |
|------|------------|
| **User** | Create posts, view calendar, use AI generation, view analytics |
| **Admin** | Everything User can do, plus: manage channels, manage team members, access billing, manage settings |
| **Super Admin** | Everything Admin can do, plus: delete the team, manage API keys, change team name |

<Note>
The account creator is automatically the **Super Admin**. This role cannot be removed or transferred.
</Note>

### What each role can do

| Action | User | Admin | Super Admin |
|--------|:----:|:-----:|:-----------:|
| Create and schedule posts | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Generate AI images/videos | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| View analytics | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Connect/manage channels | — | Yes | Yes |
| Invite/remove team members | — | Yes | Yes |
| Access billing | — | Yes | Yes |
| Manage API keys | — | — | Yes |
| Change team name | — | — | Yes |

## Managing team members

### Change a member's role

1. Find the member in the team list
2. Click the role dropdown next to their name
3. Select the new role
4. The change takes effect immediately

### Remove a member

1. Find the member in the team list
2. Click the **Remove** button
3. Confirm the removal
4. The member immediately loses access to the team

<Note>
Higher-role members can manage lower-role members. A User cannot remove an Admin, but an Admin can remove a User.
</Note>

## Shared resources

All team members share:

- **Channels** — everyone can post to the team's connected channels (based on their role)
- **Calendar** — all posts from all members appear on the same calendar
- **AI credits** — the team's credit balance is shared across all members
- **Media library** — generated and uploaded media is accessible to the team
- **Storage** — the plan's storage limit is shared
