Autopost lets you automatically import content from RSS feeds and publish it to your social channels. Connect a blog, podcast, or any RSS-compatible source, and Postzee will create posts whenever new items are detected.

## How to access

Open the account drawer (click your avatar in the top-right) → **Settings** → **Autopost** tab.

## What is Autopost?

Autopost monitors RSS feeds you configure and automatically creates posts in Postzee whenever a new item is published in the feed. You decide which channels each feed publishes to and how the posts are formatted.

Common use cases:

- **Auto-share blog posts** to your social channels when published
- **Cross-post podcast episodes** to LinkedIn and X
- **Distribute news** from external feeds to your community
- **Republish your own content** from one platform to another

## How to configure

1. Go to **Settings** → **Autopost**
2. Click **Add Autopost**
3. Enter:
   - **Title** — a friendly name for this autopost
   - **RSS URL** — the feed to monitor (Postzee validates the URL before saving)
   - **Channels** — choose **All channels** or select specific channels
4. Configure publishing options:
   - **Publish on slot** — publish at your team's configured posting times (or immediately if disabled)
   - **Sync last entries** — also import recent items already in the feed (default: only new items going forward)
   - **Generate content with AI** — let AI write the social media caption from the feed item
   - **Add picture** — attach an image extracted from the feed item
5. *(Optional)* **Custom content** — provide a template if you don't want AI-generated content
6. Click **Save**

## Channel filtering

You can scope each autopost feed to specific channels:

- **All channels** — publish every new item to every connected channel
- **Specific channels** — only publish to selected channels (useful for matching content to platform)

## AI-generated content vs custom content

By default, Autopost uses AI to generate the social media caption from each feed item, optimizing it for each platform's style and character limits.

If you prefer manual control:

- Disable **Generate content with AI**
- Provide your own template in the **Custom content** field
- Use the feed item's title and link in your template

## Validating the feed

Before saving, click **Test** to verify Postzee can read your RSS feed. The validation checks:

- The URL is reachable
- The feed is valid RSS/Atom format
- Items have the required fields

## Plan availability

Autopost is available on **Team**, **Pro**, and **Ultimate** plans.

<Note>
Autopost uses your monthly post quota. Each item published from a feed counts as one post. AI-generated content uses credits — see [Credits](/billing/credits) for details.
</Note>

## Disabling or deleting a feed

To pause autoposting:

1. Open the feed in **Settings** → **Autopost**
2. Click **Edit** → toggle off, or click **Delete** to remove permanently

Posts already in the queue are still published, but no new items will be detected after disabling.
