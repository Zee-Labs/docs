Configure your team's timezone, organization name, and other global preferences that affect how Postzee works for everyone on your team.

## How to access

Open the account drawer (click your avatar in the top-right) → **Settings** → **Preferences** tab.

## Timezone

The timezone setting affects all time-related features across your team:

- **Calendar** — posts are displayed at the correct local time
- **Scheduling** — the date/time picker uses this timezone
- **Analytics** — data is grouped by this timezone
- **Billing cycle** — credit resets align with this timezone

### Changing the timezone

1. Go to **Settings** → **Preferences**
2. Find the **Timezone** selector
3. Choose your timezone from the dropdown
4. Click **Save**

<Note>
The timezone setting applies to the entire team. All team members see times in the team's configured timezone. Individual timezone overrides are not supported — coordinate with your team when choosing a timezone.
</Note>

## Team name

Update your team's display name:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Preferences**
2. Edit the **Team Name** field
3. Click **Save**

<Note>
Changing the team name requires **Super Admin** role.
</Note>

The team name appears in:
- The navigation bar
- Team member invitations
- Email notifications

## Language

Your interface language is configured per user (not per team) in your Account settings:

- Go to **Account** → **Language** selector
- Choose between English, Portuguese (BR), Spanish, or French
- See [Account Settings](/settings/account) for details
