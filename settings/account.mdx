Manage your personal profile, password, and language preferences from the Account settings.

## How to access

Click your **avatar** in the top-right corner to open the account drawer, then click **Settings**. The Account settings page opens with tabs for each section.

## Profile information

Update your personal details:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Name** | Your first name (required, minimum 2 characters) |
| **Last name** | Your last name (optional) |
| **Bio** | A short description about you (optional) |
| **Avatar** | Upload a profile picture |
| **Phone** | Your phone number (optional) |

### Address information (optional)

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Country** | Your country |
| **State** | State or region |
| **City** | Your city |
| **Address** | Street address |
| **Zip code** | Postal code |

## Changing your password

1. Go to **Settings** → **Account**
2. Find the **Change Password** section
3. Enter your current password (if you have one set)
4. Enter your new password (minimum 6 characters)
5. Confirm and save

<Note>
If you signed up with a social login (Google, GitHub) and don't have a password set, you can add one without entering a current password.
</Note>

## Language

Change the interface language:

1. Open the **account drawer** (click your avatar)
2. Select your preferred language from the **Language** selector
3. The interface updates immediately

Supported languages:

| Language | Code |
|----------|------|
| **English** | en |
| **Portuguese (Brazil)** | pt-BR |
| **Spanish** | es |
| **French** | fr |

The language setting affects the entire Postzee interface including menus, buttons, notifications, and emails.

## Deleting your account

To request account deletion:

1. Email **support@postzee.app** from the email address registered to your account
2. Include "Account deletion request" in the subject line
3. Our team will confirm the request and process the deletion

### What happens when you delete your account

- **Subscription** — your subscription is canceled immediately. You will not be charged again.
- **Data** — all your personal data (profile, posts, generated media, analytics, channels) is permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Published posts** — content already published to your social media platforms remains there. To remove it, delete it directly on each platform.
- **Team membership** — if you are a member of other teams, you are removed from them
- **Team you own** — if you own a team with other members, contact us first to arrange ownership transfer

<Note>
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you only want to take a break, consider canceling your subscription instead — your account stays active and you can resume anytime.
</Note>

For data export requests (GDPR, LGPD), contact **support@postzee.app** before initiating deletion.
