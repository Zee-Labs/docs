## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Postzee?">
    Postzee is an AI-powered social media management platform. You can generate images and videos with AI, schedule posts to 30+ social networks, track analytics, and collaborate with your team — all from one dashboard.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many social networks does Postzee support?">
    Postzee supports 30+ platforms including Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, X (Twitter), LinkedIn, YouTube, Pinterest, Threads, Bluesky, Discord, Slack, Telegram, Reddit, Mastodon, and many more. See the [full list](/get-started/supported-platforms).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What languages does Postzee support?">
    The entire interface is available in **English**, **Portuguese (BR)**, **Spanish**, and **French**. You can change the language in the account drawer → **Language** selector.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

## Billing & Credits

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Am I going to be charged right away?">
    New users on paid plans start with a **7-day free trial**. No charge during the trial period. After the trial, billing begins automatically. You can cancel anytime. If you've used a trial before, billing starts immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are AI credits?">
    AI credits are the currency used to generate images and videos with AI. Each generation costs a specific number of credits depending on the model. Credits can be included in your plan (monthly allocation) or purchased separately. See [Credits](/billing/credits) for details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do purchased credits expire?">
    **No.** Purchased credits never expire and survive plan cancellation. Only monthly plan credits reset each billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use AI features on the Free plan?">
    Yes! Free plan users can **purchase credits** and use all AI features (image generation, video generation, prompt optimization). The Free plan only restricts access to the social media suite (scheduling, publishing, channels).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I run out of credits?">
    You can purchase additional credit packages anytime from the **Credits** page, or upgrade to a plan with more monthly credits. Your scheduled posts continue to work normally — only AI generation requires credits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel my subscription at any time?">
    Yes. You can cancel anytime with no cancellation fees. When you cancel, you keep access to your plan's features until the end of the paid period. Your purchased credits are never lost.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are the plan differences?">
    - **Free** ($0): AI features only (buy credits), no social suite
    - **Standard** ($27/mo): 5 channels, 400 posts/month, 2,000 AI credits
    - **Team** ($37/mo): 10 channels, unlimited posts, unlimited team, 3,000 AI credits
    - **Pro** ($47/mo): 30 channels, unlimited posts, unlimited team, 5,000 AI credits
    - **Ultimate** ($97/mo): 100 channels, unlimited everything, 10,000 AI credits

    All paid plans include the full social media suite, AI features, and API access. See [Plans](/billing/plans) for the complete comparison.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Channels & Publishing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are channels?">
    A channel is a connected social media account where you can publish content. For example, your Instagram business profile or your LinkedIn company page. Connect channels in the **Channels** page by clicking **Add Channel**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I connect multiple accounts for the same platform?">
    Yes! You can connect multiple Instagram accounts, multiple LinkedIn pages, etc. Each connection counts as one channel toward your plan's limit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are team members?">
    Team members are people you invite to your workspace. They can create content, manage channels, and collaborate on posts. Each member can connect their own channels. Available on **Team**, **Pro**, and **Ultimate** plans.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my data safe?">
    Yes. Postzee uses end-to-end encryption, secure servers, LGPD/GDPR compliance, and automatic daily backups. Social network access tokens are stored securely and never shared with third parties.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## AI & Content Creation

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What AI models are available?">
    Postzee offers 16 image models (Nano Banana, FLUX, Recraft, Ideogram, GPT Image) and 30+ video models (Sora 2, Veo 3.1, Kling, Luma Ray, Seedance, Wan, MiniMax, PixVerse, Vidu, Hunyuan, and more). See the full catalog in [Image Models](/ai-models/image-models) and [Video Models](/ai-models/video-models).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the Prompt Optimizer?">
    The Prompt Optimizer automatically transforms your simple descriptions into professional-grade prompts. It adds camera specifications, lighting design, color grading, textures, and artistic style — dramatically improving the quality of generated images and videos. It's free to use (no credit cost).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use Postzee with AI agents?">
    Yes! Postzee has an MCP server that works with **Claude Code** and **OpenClaw**. You can generate images, videos, and publish posts entirely through conversation with your AI agent. See [AI Agents](/integrations/mcp/overview).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Integrations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does Postzee have an API?">
    Yes. Postzee offers a public REST API for programmatic access. You can create posts, generate media, manage channels, and more. See the [API Reference](/api-reference/authentication).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I integrate Postzee with n8n?">
    Yes! Postzee has a custom n8n node with 5 operations: Create Post, Generate Image, Generate Video, List Channels, and Get Credits. See the [n8n Integration](/integrations/n8n/overview) guide.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
