Connect your TikTok account to Postzee to publish videos directly from the dashboard. TikTok requires specific content disclosure settings that Postzee handles for you.

## Requirements

- A **TikTok** account
- Content must be in video format (TikTok does not support image-only posts via API)

## How to connect

1. Go to **Channels** in the top navigation bar
2. Click **Add Channel** → **TikTok**
3. Log in with your TikTok account
4. Authorize Postzee
5. Configure the required content disclosure settings
6. Your TikTok channel appears in the channel list

## Content disclosure settings

TikTok's API requires specific disclosure settings on every post. These are configured per channel and can be overridden per post:

| Setting | Options | Description |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Title** | Text (max 90 characters) | Video title/description |
| **Privacy** | Public, Mutual Follow Friends, Followers Only, Private | Who can see your video |
| **Allow Duets** | On / Off | Whether others can create duets |
| **Allow Stitches** | On / Off | Whether others can stitch your video |
| **Allow Comments** | On / Off | Whether viewers can comment |
| **Auto-add Music** | Yes / No | Let TikTok add background music |
| **Brand Content** | On / Off | Disclose branded/sponsored content |
| **Made with AI** | On / Off | Disclose AI-generated content |

<Warning>
These disclosure settings are required by TikTok's API compliance rules and cannot be skipped. Postzee validates them before every publish to ensure compliance.
</Warning>

## Supported content types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Video** | Vertical video (9:16 recommended) |

## Tips

- **Aspect ratio:** Always use 9:16 (portrait) for TikTok — it's designed for vertical viewing.
- **Duration:** Videos up to 10 minutes are supported, but 15-60 seconds tends to perform best.
- **AI disclosure:** If your video was generated with AI, enable the "Made with AI" toggle to comply with TikTok's policies.
