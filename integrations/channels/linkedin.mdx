Connect your LinkedIn personal profile or Company Page to Postzee to publish posts, images, articles, and carousel content.

## Requirements

- A **LinkedIn** account
- For Company Pages: admin access to the page

## How to connect

1. Go to **Channels** in the top navigation bar
2. Click **Add Channel** → **LinkedIn**
3. Log in with your LinkedIn account
4. Choose between your **Personal Profile** or a **Company Page**
5. Authorize the required permissions
6. Your LinkedIn channel appears in the channel list

<Note>
You can connect both your personal profile and company pages as separate channels. Each counts toward your plan's channel limit.
</Note>

## Supported content types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Text post** | Text-only status update |
| **Image post** | Single or multiple images |
| **Image carousel** | Swipeable image carousel (toggle in post settings) |
| **Video** | Native video upload |
| **Article** | Long-form article with rich formatting |

## Platform-specific settings

| Setting | Options | Description |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Image Carousel** | On / Off | Display multiple images as a swipeable carousel |

## Tips

- **Carousel posts:** Toggle the carousel setting to create swipeable LinkedIn posts — great for educational content, step-by-step guides, and presentations.
- **Aspect ratios:** Landscape (16:9) and square (1:1) work well. Portrait (4:5) is also supported.
- **Character limit:** LinkedIn allows up to 3,000 characters per post.
- **Short links:** Postzee can automatically shorten URLs in LinkedIn posts for cleaner formatting and click tracking.
