Connect your YouTube channel to Postzee to upload videos and Shorts with titles, descriptions, thumbnails, and visibility settings.

## Requirements

- A **Google account** with a **YouTube channel**

## How to connect

1. Go to **Channels** in the top navigation bar
2. Click **Add Channel** → **YouTube**
3. Log in with your Google account
4. Select the YouTube channel you want to manage
5. Authorize the required permissions
6. Your YouTube channel appears in the channel list

## Supported content types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Video** | Standard video upload with title, description, and tags |
| **Short** | Vertical short-form video (9:16, up to 60 seconds) |

## Platform-specific settings

| Setting | Options | Description |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Title** | Text (2-100 characters) | Video title — required for all YouTube uploads |
| **Visibility** | Public, Private, Unlisted | Who can see your video |
| **Thumbnail** | Image upload | Custom video thumbnail |
| **Tags** | Text tags | Searchable keywords for discovery |

## Tips

- **Title is required:** YouTube requires a title for every video. Postzee will prompt you to add one if you forget.
- **Thumbnails:** Custom thumbnails significantly improve click-through rates. Upload a 1280x720 image.
- **Aspect ratios:** Use 16:9 for standard videos and 9:16 for Shorts.
- **Tags:** Add relevant tags to help YouTube's algorithm recommend your content.
