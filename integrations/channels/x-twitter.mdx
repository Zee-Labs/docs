Connect your X (formerly Twitter) account to Postzee to publish tweets, threads, and media from the dashboard.

## Requirements

- An **X (Twitter)** account

## How to connect

1. Go to **Channels** in the top navigation bar
2. Click **Add Channel** → **X (Twitter)**
3. Log in with your X account
4. Authorize Postzee
5. Your X channel appears in the channel list

## Supported content types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Tweet** | Text post (up to 280 characters) |
| **Thread** | Multiple connected tweets |
| **Image tweet** | Tweet with up to 4 images |
| **Video tweet** | Tweet with a video attachment |

## Tips

- **Character limit:** 280 characters per tweet. Postzee warns you if your content exceeds this limit.
- **Threads:** Create multi-tweet threads for longer content. Each tweet in the thread is connected automatically.
- **Images:** Up to 4 images per tweet. Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, GIF, WEBP.
- **Aspect ratios:** Landscape (16:9) and square (1:1) display best in the timeline.
