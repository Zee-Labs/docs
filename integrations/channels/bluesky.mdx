Connect your Bluesky account to Postzee to publish posts and images to the AT Protocol-based social network.

## Requirements

- A **Bluesky** account

## How to connect

1. Go to **Channels** in the top navigation bar
2. Click **Add Channel** → **Bluesky**
3. Enter your Bluesky credentials
4. Your Bluesky channel appears in the channel list

## Supported content types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Text post** | Text-only post (up to 300 characters) |
| **Image post** | Post with images |

## Tips

- **Character limit:** Bluesky allows up to 300 characters per post.
- **AT Protocol:** Bluesky uses the decentralized AT Protocol. Your posts are published to the Bluesky network.
- **Images:** Supported formats include JPEG and PNG.
