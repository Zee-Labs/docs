Connect your Facebook Pages and Groups to Postzee to publish posts, photos, and videos from the dashboard.

## Requirements

- A **Facebook Page** or **Group** you manage
- A **Facebook account** with admin access

## How to connect

1. Go to **Channels** in the top navigation bar
2. Click **Add Channel** → **Facebook**
3. Log in with your Facebook account
4. Select the **Page** or **Group** you want to connect
5. Authorize the required permissions
6. Your Facebook channel appears in the channel list

## Supported content types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Text post** | Text-only status update |
| **Photo** | Single or multiple image post |
| **Video** | Video upload to your Page or Group |
| **Link** | Post with a URL and link preview |

## Tips

- **Video:** Facebook favors native video uploads over links. Upload videos directly for better reach.
- **Aspect ratios:** Landscape (16:9) and square (1:1) work well for feed posts.
- **Pages vs Groups:** Both are supported as separate channels. Each counts toward your plan's channel limit.
