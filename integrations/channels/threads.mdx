Connect your Threads account to Postzee to publish text posts and images to Meta's conversation platform.

## Requirements

- A **Threads** account (linked to your Instagram profile)

## How to connect

1. Go to **Channels** in the top navigation bar
2. Click **Add Channel** → **Threads**
3. Log in with your Meta account
4. Authorize Postzee
5. Your Threads channel appears in the channel list

## Supported content types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Text post** | Text-only post (up to 500 characters) |
| **Image post** | Post with one or more images |

## Tips

- **Character limit:** Threads allows up to 500 characters per post.
- **Cross-posting:** Threads works well when combined with Instagram — schedule the same content to both platforms simultaneously.
- **Images:** Square (1:1) and portrait (4:5) formats display best.
