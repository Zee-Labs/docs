Connect your Pinterest account to Postzee to create Pins with images, descriptions, and board targeting.

## Requirements

- A **Pinterest** account (personal or business)

## How to connect

1. Go to **Channels** in the top navigation bar
2. Click **Add Channel** → **Pinterest**
3. Log in with your Pinterest account
4. Authorize Postzee
5. Your Pinterest channel appears in the channel list

## Supported content types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Pin** | Image pin with description and link URL |

## Tips

- **Aspect ratio:** Vertical images (2:3) perform best on Pinterest.
- **Link URL:** Add a destination URL to drive traffic to your website or landing page.
- **Board selection:** Choose which board to pin to when creating a post.
- **Descriptions:** Use keyword-rich descriptions for Pinterest search discovery.
