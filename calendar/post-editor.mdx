The Post Editor is where you compose, customize, and publish your social media content. Write once, customize per platform, and publish to multiple channels simultaneously.

## Opening the Post Editor

You can open the Post Editor from several places:

- **Calendar** — click on a date or an existing post
- **Zee Studio** — select **Create Post** mode, or click **Create Post** on a generated image/video

## Writing your post

### Content field

The main text editor supports:

- **Rich text** with platform-appropriate formatting
- **Up to 2,200 characters** — a counter shows remaining characters
- **Emoji support** — use the emoji picker or type emoji directly
- **Hashtag suggestions** — popular hashtags by category (marketing, business, technology, lifestyle)

<Note>
Character limits vary by platform. Instagram allows 2,200 characters, X (Twitter) allows 280, LinkedIn allows 3,000. Postzee will warn you if your content exceeds a platform's limit.
</Note>

### Attaching media

Add images and videos to your post:

- **From Zee Studio** — click **Create Post** on any generated image or video
- **From Files** — select uploaded files from your library
- **Direct upload** — drag and drop or click to upload new files

Supported media types depend on the platform:

| Platform | Single Image | Multiple Images | Video | Carousel |
|----------|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| Instagram | Yes | Yes | Yes (Reels) | Yes |
| Facebook | Yes | Yes | Yes | — |
| X (Twitter) | Yes | Yes (up to 4) | Yes | — |
| LinkedIn | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| TikTok | — | — | Yes | — |
| YouTube | — | — | Yes | — |
| Pinterest | Yes | — | — | — |
| Threads | Yes | Yes | — | — |

## Selecting channels

Choose which channels receive this post:

1. In the channel selector, check the channels you want to publish to
2. Selected channels show a checkmark and the count updates (e.g., "Channels (3)")
3. Each channel shows its avatar, name, and platform

You can select channels from different platforms — Postzee handles the format differences automatically.

<Note>
Only enabled channels appear in the selector. If a channel needs reconnection, it will show as disabled. Go to **Channels** to fix the connection.
</Note>

## Platform-specific settings

Some platforms require or support additional settings. These appear when you select a channel from that platform:

### TikTok

| Setting | Options |
|---------|---------|
| **Title** | Up to 90 characters |
| **Privacy** | Public, Mutual Follow Friends, Followers Only, Private |
| **Allow Duets** | On / Off |
| **Allow Stitches** | On / Off |
| **Allow Comments** | On / Off |
| **Auto-add Music** | Yes / No |
| **Brand Content** | Toggle for branded/sponsored content disclosure |
| **Made with AI** | Toggle for AI-generated content disclosure |

### YouTube

| Setting | Options |
|---------|---------|
| **Title** | 2-100 characters (required) |
| **Visibility** | Public, Private, Unlisted |
| **Thumbnail** | Upload a custom thumbnail image |
| **Tags** | Add searchable tags |

### Instagram

| Setting | Options |
|---------|---------|
| **Post Type** | Feed Post or Story |
| **Collaborators** | Tag collaborator accounts |

### LinkedIn

| Setting | Options |
|---------|---------|
| **Carousel** | Toggle to display multiple images as a carousel |

## Tags

Organize your posts with tags for easy filtering:

1. In the post editor, click the **Tags** field
2. Select existing tags or create new ones
3. Tags appear as colored chips on your post
4. Use tags to filter posts in the Calendar view

Tags are shared across your team — anyone can create, edit, or filter by tags.

## Short links

Postzee can automatically shorten URLs in your posts for cleaner formatting and click tracking:

- Toggle **Short Links** in the post editor
- Shortened links are tracked — view click statistics in the post details

## Content from Zee Studio

The fastest way to create posts with AI content:

1. Generate an image or video in **Zee Studio**
2. Click **Create Post** on the generated media
3. The post editor opens with the media already attached
4. Write your caption, select channels, and schedule

You can also use the **Create Post** mode directly in Zee Studio to write your post alongside AI generation tools.
