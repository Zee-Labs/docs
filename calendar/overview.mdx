The **Calendar** is your command center for social media content. See all your posts at a glance, drag to reschedule, filter by channel or status, and manage your entire content pipeline from one view.

## How to access

Click **Calendar** in the left sidebar navigation. Your posts are displayed on a visual calendar with color-coded statuses.

## Calendar views

The calendar supports four views, selectable from the toolbar:

| View | What it shows |
|------|--------------|
| **Month** | Full month grid with up to 3 posts per day (default on desktop) |
| **Week** | 7-day view with hourly time slots |
| **Day** | Single day with detailed hourly breakdown |
| **Agenda** | Chronological list of upcoming posts (default on mobile) |

<Note>
On mobile devices, the calendar automatically switches to **Agenda** view for easier reading. On desktop, it defaults to **Month** view.
</Note>

## Post status colors

Each post on the calendar is color-coded by its current status:

| Color | Status | Meaning |
|-------|--------|---------|
| **Gray** | Draft | Saved but not scheduled |
| **Blue** | Scheduled | Queued to publish at a specific date/time |
| **Purple** | Published | Successfully posted to the platform |
| **Red** | Error | Failed to publish — action needed |

## Interacting with the calendar

### View a post

Click on any post in the calendar to open the post details. From there you can view the content, edit, reschedule, or delete.

### Drag to reschedule

Drag a scheduled post from one date/time to another to instantly reschedule it. This works in Month, Week, and Day views.

<Note>
Only **Draft** and **Scheduled** posts can be rescheduled by dragging. Published and Error posts are locked in place.
</Note>

### Create a new post

Click on any date in the calendar to open the post editor with that date pre-selected. Or use the **Create Post** button in the toolbar.

### Navigate dates

- Use the **left/right arrows** in the toolbar to move between months, weeks, or days
- Click **Today** to jump back to the current date

## Filtering

Use the filter controls above the calendar to narrow your view:

| Filter | Options |
|--------|---------|
| **Channel** | Show posts for specific channels only |
| **Status** | Filter by Draft, Scheduled, Published, or Error |
| **Tags** | Filter by content tags you've assigned |

Active filters show as chips that you can clear individually, or use **Reset filters** to remove all.

## Recurring post groups

If you have recurring posts (posts that repeat on a set interval), they appear with matching color indicators on the calendar. A legend below the calendar shows:

- Color indicator for each recurring group
- Group title and post count
- Date range of the recurring series

Hover over a group in the legend to highlight all its posts on the calendar. Click to navigate to a specific group.

## Key features

- **Week starts on Monday** — the calendar grid always starts on Monday
- **Timezone-aware** — all times are displayed in your team's configured timezone
- **Responsive** — adapts layout for desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Multilingual** — calendar labels and dates respect your language setting (English, Portuguese, Spanish, French)
